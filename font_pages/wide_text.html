How it works

This tool converts ordinary Latin letters, numbers, spaces, and common punctuation into Unicode fullwidth characters.

Click any output box to copy the result. Then paste it into Facebook, Instagram, X, Threads, or wherever else you want your text to stand out.

Wide text is one of those rare Unicode tricks that is both useful and gloriously extra.
