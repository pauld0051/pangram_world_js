Alternating Text Generator

Convert plain text into alternating lower upper and upper lower patterns across multiple Unicode styles.
Quick examples

How it works

This tool first alternates the casing of your text, then applies the selected style. Standard outputs stay as normal letters with no Unicode styling.

Click any output box or copy button to copy the result.

Some styles do not support every character equally across all devices and fonts, because Unicode enjoys occasional acts of sabotage.
