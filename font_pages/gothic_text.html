How it works

This tool converts ordinary Latin letters into Unicode Fraktur styles, including standard Fraktur, bold Fraktur, and a few alternating variants for decorative use.

Click any output box to copy the result. Then paste it into Facebook, Instagram, X, Threads, Discord, or wherever else you want your text to look like it owns a suspiciously large castle.

Some Unicode styles do not support every character equally, so a few letters may fall back to normal text depending on the platform or font. Unicode remains a delightful little gremlin.
