How it works

This tool converts ordinary Latin letters and numbers into Unicode mathematical characters that look italic or bold italic.

Click any output box to copy the result. Then paste it into Facebook, Instagram, X, Threads, or wherever else you want your text to stand out.

Not every platform or font renders every Unicode character perfectly, because Unicode likes a little drama.
