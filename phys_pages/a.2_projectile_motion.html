About this calculator

Built for classroom physics

This version covers the standard no-drag projectile questions you see in IB and introductory mechanics without turning into a numerical methods dissertation.

Flexible launch setup

It handles level launches, launches from height, and landing on a higher platform, provided the launch conditions actually make physical sense.

Version 1 boundary

Air resistance is deliberately not included here. Once drag shows up, the tidy equations leave the building and numerical solving starts asking for its own room.
