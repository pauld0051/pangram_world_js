Welcome to Pangram-World

Fantasy books, physics tools, useful symbols, and a site built to save people time.

This is the home of P.S. Davis: fantasy author, physics teacher, and builder of practical online tools. You can explore epic fantasy books, browse symbols and alphabets, generate styled text, use physics equations and calculators, or simply wander about and click shiny things in an orderly fashion.

A practical site with a fantasy heart

Some visitors come for the books. Some arrive needing a Greek letter, a clean bit of physics notation, a Roman numeral, or a faster way to format text without wrestling software into submission. Either way, you are in the right place.

Everything here is designed to be useful, clear, and easy to copy, paste, read, or explore. Less friction. Less nonsense. More getting on with the actual work.

Why people use this site

Because many online tools are cluttered, awkward, overbuilt, or trying to sell you seventeen things before letting you copy one symbol. This site aims to be cleaner, quicker, and far less annoying.

Built by a real human

P.S. Davis is a fantasy author and physics teacher. That odd combination turns out to be very useful when building a site full of stories, equations, alphabets, and practical tools.
