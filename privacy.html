Privacy Policy

Privacy matters here

Pangram World is designed to be simple, useful, and respectful of your privacy. I do not run tracking tools, advertising platforms, login systems, or sales funnels on this site.

This site is built to minimise data collection. If all you do is browse pages and use the tools, I do not try to profile you, sell your data, or follow you around the internet like an overexcited mall kiosk.

This policy explains what is and is not collected, how limited technical data may be handled, and how to get in touch if you have privacy questions.

If hosting, security, or email services process limited technical data behind the scenes, that is kept to what is reasonably necessary to operate and protect the site.

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to visitors using Pangram World and related pages on this site.

What I do not collect on purpose

As a rule, this site does not intentionally collect personal information through:

  • tracking cookies
  • advertising identifiers
  • analytics or behavioural profiling tools
  • user accounts or login systems
  • checkout systems or payment forms
  • marketing automation or sales platforms

Technical data that may still exist

Like most websites, the site may still involve limited technical processing at the hosting, server, content delivery, or security level. This can include information such as IP addresses, browser details, requested pages, timestamps, or similar basic log data needed to deliver, secure, and troubleshoot the service.

I do not use that kind of data to build marketing profiles or track your activity across other sites.

Cookies and similar technologies

Pangram World is intended to operate without non-essential cookies or similar tracking technologies.

If strictly necessary technical cookies are ever used by the hosting environment or by core site functionality, they would only be used to make the service work properly, not to track you for advertising or analytics.

Email contact

If you email me at p.s.davis@psdavisbooks.com, I will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, your message, and any details included in it.

I will only use that information to respond to you, continue the conversation you started, or deal with the issue you raised. I do not add people to mailing lists without consent.

External links

This site includes links to external services such as Amazon, Goodreads, Facebook, and Instagram. If you click those links, you will leave this site and move to platforms with their own privacy practices.

I am not responsible for how those third-party sites collect or use data once you are on their pages.

Legal basis and privacy rights

Where limited personal data is processed, such as basic technical data needed to run the site or information you send by email, the aim is to keep that processing limited, proportionate, and tied to operating the site or answering your message.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights relating to your personal data, including rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or more information about how data is handled.

To ask a privacy question or make a request, contact: p.s.davis@psdavisbooks.com

Data retention

I do not keep personal data longer than reasonably necessary for the purpose it was received.

Email messages and essential technical records may be retained for as long as needed to respond, resolve issues, maintain security, or meet legal obligations.

Children's privacy

This site is intended as a general-use educational and writing resource. It does not knowingly collect personal data from children through registrations, profiles, or marketing systems because none of those features exist here.
