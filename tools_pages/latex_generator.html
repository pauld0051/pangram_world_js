What this tool does

This page is designed for rendering equation snippets as PNG images. It is not a full LaTeX editor and it does not aim to replace Overleaf or a full document workflow.

Proper LaTeX input will be the most reliable. Very simple plain-text equations may be converted in basic cases, but complex expressions, matrices, aligned lines, custom macros, or package-dependent syntax should be entered directly in LaTeX.

KaTeX is fast and excellent for maths, though it does not support every LaTeX command under the sun. Like most things in life, it's nearly perfect.

Background choices are transparent or white, and size controls how large the rendered equation will be before export.
