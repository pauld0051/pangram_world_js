What this tool does

This converter auto-detects whether your input is a standard Roman numeral or a whole number, then fills in both outputs.

It supports whole numbers from 1 to 3999 only. It does not support zero, negative values, decimal points, fractions, or overline notation for values above 3999.

Roman numeral input must be in standard form. So XIV, XCIX, and MCMXCIX will work, while IIII, VX, and IC will be rejected.

The Romans built roads, aqueducts, and empires. Zero did not make the cut.
