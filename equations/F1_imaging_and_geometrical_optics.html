Why this page is useful

Strong cross-curriculum topic

Imaging and geometrical optics show up across older IB material and many other school courses, so this page fills a genuine gap rather than padding the site.

Actually useful in class

Students can grab the lens, magnification, fibre-optics, attenuation, and medical-imaging equations without rebuilding fractions, logs, and subscripts by hand.

Helpful extras

Alongside the old booklet set, the page adds refraction, combined lens powers, acoustic reflection, and resolution helpers that teachers genuinely use.
