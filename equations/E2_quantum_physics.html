Why this page is useful

Fast classroom use

Students and teachers can grab the key quantum equations for photons, matter waves, Compton scattering, and quantum thresholds without rebuilding fractions, roots, and symbols by hand.

Cleaner documents

The social-text copies already include symbols like λ, Φ, Δ, and subscripts, which makes notes, slides, and posts look far less clunky.

Useful extras

Alongside the booklet equations, the page adds photon-momentum, stopping-potential, mass-energy, uncertainty, and particle-number helpers that are genuinely handy in class.
