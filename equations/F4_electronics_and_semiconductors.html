Why this page is useful

Bridges circuits into electronics

This page goes past basic current-and-circuits work and gives you the genuinely useful school-level electronics relations in one place.

Helpful for sensors and control

Potential dividers with LDRs and thermistors, LED resistor sizing, and transistor current gain are exactly the sort of things students build and test.

Logic gates included

Logic gates are not classical equations in the usual sense, but the Boolean forms are useful and fit the same one-card, one-idea system nicely.
