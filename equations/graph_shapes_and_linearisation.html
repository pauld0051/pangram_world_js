Tips that save marks

Do not force the wrong axes

If the graph is curved, change the variables. Straight lines are where the useful physics usually lives.

Label transformed axes clearly

Write t², 1/V, ln A, or ln x properly. A sloppy axis can wreck a perfectly good graph.

Know what the slope means

The line is only half the job. The gradient and intercept are usually the actual physical targets.
